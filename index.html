Frequently asked questions

Some tips about NECC, the Hydrogen Energy Blockchain and Energy Mining.

What is NECC ?

NECC is the Renewable Hydrogen Energy Trading System, is launched globally by the World Hydrogen Energy Foundation to create a decentralized renewable hydrogen energy alliance ecosystem based on advanced and mature hydrogen energy extraction and storage technologies, combined with blockchain. The alliance chain ecosystem includes users, research institutes under the World Hydrogen Energy Foundation, leading hydrogen energy companies in each country, application developers, auditors, notaries, supervisors, etc.

Why NECC?

from the perspective of technology and production, the technology of lithium battery is mature and the industrial chain is perfect, and it can be mass-produced; while hydrogen fuel cell is more difficult to develop and manufacture, and the key technologies are mainly mastered in Europe, America, Japan and a few other countries, and the related industry chain in other countries is basically in a blank state.

How can blockchain technology help you get a head start on the new energy track?

New energy vehicles are favored by blockchain because of their advantages such as smart, electrification, connectivity and shareability, and it seems that blockchain has already deeply penetrated into the field of smart electric vehicles.
